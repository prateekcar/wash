About Us

Prateek's Story

Founded in 2018, Prateek Car Washing has been providing top-quality vehicle cleaning services across Amritsar. What started as a small mobile washing service has grown into a trusted name for car owners who value cleanliness and convenience.

Our mission is simple: deliver exceptional cleaning services that make your vehicle look brand new, while saving you time and effort. We use eco-friendly products and modern techniques to ensure the best results every time.
